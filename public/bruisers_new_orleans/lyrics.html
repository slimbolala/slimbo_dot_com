Lyrics

Remember back when albums had the lyrics in their liner notes, and you could sit and read them while listening to the songs? Then came cassettes, and the packaging was smaller, and lyrics sometimes fell by the wayside. CDs were still worse. And MP3s? Forget about it. But we like reading lyrics, and we thought maybe you'd like to read ours. Here are some. And we'll be be adding more soon.
